Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions

What is this screening tool?

This is an informational screening tool designed to help parents identify early signs of ASD, ADHD, and Dyslexia. It is not a diagnostic tool and does not replace professional medical evaluation.

How accurate are the results?

The screening questions are based on established diagnostic criteria, but results should be interpreted as preliminary indicators only. A comprehensive professional evaluation is necessary for diagnosis.

How long does screening take?

Each screening typically takes 10-15 minutes to complete. You can save your progress and return later if needed.

Is my data private?

Yes, your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. We follow strict privacy and data protection standards.

What should I do after screening?

If the screening indicates potential concerns, we recommend consulting with a qualified healthcare professional for comprehensive evaluation. We provide therapist recommendations to help you find appropriate support.

Can I screen multiple children?

Yes! You can add multiple child profiles and track screenings for each child separately. This helps you monitor their development over time.
